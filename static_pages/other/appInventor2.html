App Inventor 2

App Inventor 2 is in preview mode but can be accessed at http://ai2.appinventor.mit.edu. It is fabulous and provides a much more elegant programming experience with little setup. It is fairly stable but the App Inventor team offers no promises at this time-- use it at your own risk! If I, David Wolber, were teaching a course, I'd use it, but I have a lot of experience teaching App Inventor and I'm also a little crazy.

If you're a developer or checking out App Inventor for future use, you should absolutely use it. Just be sure and save your source frequently by choosing More Actions | Download Source on the My Projects page. Note that the source files have an ".aia" extension instead of ".zip".
